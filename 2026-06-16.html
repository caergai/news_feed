AGI and Timeline

The loop has begun: AI is now building better versions of itself.

Artificial intelligence is entering the era of recursive self-improvement (RSI), creating a closed loop of autonomous development. In this process, version one of a model produces version two, which then further improves itself. This shift reduces the reliance on human-led iterations to drive model capabilities. By automating the building process, AI can potentially accelerate its own intelligence gains exponentially.

Recursive self-improvement transforms AI from a human-built tool into an engine of autonomous, exponential growth.
